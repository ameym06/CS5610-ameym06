Future Scope of the Project

The sole purpose of this implementing this project was LEARNING. My aim during the implementation was to cover as many topics (that I learned in class) as possible. I stuck with the MEAN Stack web application. It is only after the completion of the project, that we can think of its future scope. After the completion of project, I realized that this project can be enhanced in following ways :

  • The Music and Videos services related data can be made persistent. Meaning, the users could save their favorite songs / videos
  • The 'My Blogs' page could be enhanced into 'My Profile' page wherein the users could also see their favorite songs / videos
  • The Users could view other users' favorite lists and share / recommend related music / videos based on genres and channels resp.

I would definitely like to enhance my project and implement all the above functionality.
